About the UMS

Welcome to the UBC UMS! We host social events for students in the math department and sell exam packages for math courses.

Come visit our spacious lounge in MATX 1119! We have couches, chalkboards, and board games (so many board games). Hungry? We sell snacks and drinks! And if you're lucky, there might even be free pizza.

Join the UMS

You don't have to be a math major to join, though many of us like talking about math. Visit the lounge or follow our Facebook page to see when the next event is.
