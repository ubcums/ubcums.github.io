Frequently Asked Questions

Where is the UMS lounge?

Math Annex, 1119.

When is the UMS open?

Regular hours: Monday to Friday, 10am - 4am.

Regular hours apply during winter semester, not including finals season and school holidays. For exact dates for this year, see the UBC Calendar pages here and here.

We also sell exam packs in finals season and throughout the summer; however we are not always staffed during this time. Please make an appointment to buy your exam pack, and we'll make sure someone is there to assist you.

What payment methods are accepted?

We take cash, credit (MasterCard, Visa, and American Express), and Interac e-Transfer. Please note that credit charges come with a small surcharge.

What happened to the Math Club?

The Undergraduate Mathematics Society used to be known as the UBC Math Club. We rebranded as a society in August 2019 to reflect our status as a department-associated academic organization, rather than an AMS club.
